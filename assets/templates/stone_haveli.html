The Stone Haveli

Located in the heritage zones of the Walled City, the Stone Haveli is an exercise in Material Honesty. Here, the air carries the scent of damp stone and aged cedar. We reject the high-gloss finish of modern hospitality; instead, surfaces of raw basalt and untarnished brass are allowed to age and patina, honoring the belief that the marks of use add value rather than diminish it.

Material Honesty

We believe in the beauty of wear. Surfaces are not polished to erase history but left to oxidize, mirroring the city's own relationship with time.

Scent & Memory

There is no bustling lobby. Arrival is signaled simply by the weight of a heavy brass key and a silver tray holding a single cup of hand-pressed tea.
